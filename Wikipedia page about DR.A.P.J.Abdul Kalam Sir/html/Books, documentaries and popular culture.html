Popular culture:

In the 2011 Hindi film I Am Kalam, Kalam is portrayed as a positive influence on a poor but bright Rajasthani boy named Chhotu, who renames himself Kalam in honour of his idol.[222] My Hero Kalam is a 2018 Indian Kannada-language biographical film by Shivu Hiremath which portrays his life from childhood to the Pokhran tests.[223] People's President is a 2016 Indian documentary feature film directed by Pankaj Vyas which covers the life of Kalam. It was produced by the Government of India's Films Division.[224] Mega Icons (2018–2020), a documentary television series about prominent personalities of India which aired on National Geographic, based the third episode – "APJ Abdul Kalam" – on Kalam's life and his ascendancy to India's presidency.[225] Soorarai Pottru, a 2020 film about the Indian aviation industry had a look-alike of Kalam, Sheik Maideen, portraying him.[226] Rocket Boys[227], an Indian Hindi-language Biographical streaming television series on SonyLIV. The character of Kalam was played by Arjun Radhakrishnan.
